How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?

  • Recycle
  • Renewable Energy
To minimize the impact, companies that produce computers can use more renewable sources of energy to lessen any harm that they may be doing to the environment. We can also properly recycle our computers so that those computers can reuse computer parts and resources to prevent more depletion of resources and minimize e-waste.

What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use?

  • Brightness
  • Power-Saving Mode
We can reduce the environmental impact by being on our computer less, therefore using less energy. Uomputer functions like power-saving mode and turning down the brightness also save energy, reducing the impact.

How can we minimize the impact of computer device disposal on the environment?

  • Recycle
  • Educate
To do this, we can educate computer users on how to handle their devices properly, use it the right way and recycle them correctly. By educating the user of the computer we will prevent lots of possible improper disposals of computers since they now know how to do it the right way.
