Harmful Effects Reducing Impact on the Environment

How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?

  • Recycle
  • Renewable Energy
To minimize the impact, companies that produce computers can use more renewable sources of energy to lessen any harm that they may be doing to the environment. We can also properly recycle our computers so that those computers can reuse computer parts and resources to prevent more depletion of resources and minimize e-waste.

What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use?

  • Brightness
  • Power-Saving Mode
